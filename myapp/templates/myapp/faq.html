Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about ScrapSort.

What is ScrapSort?
ScrapSort is a web app that detects waste items from images and provides actionable disposal and recycling guidance.
How does it detect waste?
We use the YOLOv8 model to identify the most likely material class, then generate guidance tailored to that class.
How effective is it for people?
It simplifies decision-making by recommending Reduce/Reuse/Recycle/Responsible Disposal, reducing contamination and improving recycling participation.
What is the model's accuracy?
Accuracy varies by material and image quality. We show a confidence score for transparency and recommend verifying locally for critical decisions.
How does this solve a real problem?
Mixed waste and poor sorting reduce recycling efficiency. ScrapSort guides users to handle items correctly, reducing landfill burden and emissions.
